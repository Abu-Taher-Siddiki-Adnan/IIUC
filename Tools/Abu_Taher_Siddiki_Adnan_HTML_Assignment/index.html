Welcome to My Recipe Blog

One of my favorite dishes is Mandi Biriyani. Originating from the Arabian Peninsula, it's a fragrant rice dish made with tender meat—usually chicken or lamb—slow-cooked with a blend of spices and served over smoky, spiced basmati rice. The unique cooking method and earthy flavors make it a true feast, perfect for special gatherings and celebrations!
